This time, I attended the 2026 IEEE RoboSoft Conference in Kanazawa, where I work, but with a twist. I was the Chair of the Art Gallery and part of the local organizing committee.

It was my first time doing this, and I was busy all day!

<img src='https://1drv.ms/i/c/8fdb81ef8eb9e673/IQTgndmEXEs5RYgqGd4m5S9YAd6FE9mYqdh4CJw56_PDM_k?width=1000&height=667' width="80%">

The gallery featured many interesting pieces, including works not only from robotic artists but also from the art field. The space was open to the public, so even people passing by could stop and enjoy.

It was a great success (at least for me). The reception hour was crowded, and I felt really happy seeing everything come together.
