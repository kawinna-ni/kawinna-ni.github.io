This work presents the development of an Internet of Things (IoT)-integrated wheelchair designed to enhance user safety and assistive functionality. The system incorporates sensors and network connectivity to monitor the wheelchair’s status and environment, enabling real-time safety features such as hazard detection and remote monitoring. The proposed approach improves user independence while reducing risks associated with mobility, demonstrating the potential of IoT technologies in assistive healthcare devices.
