Dressing and undressing are basic tasks that many individuals such as disabled, injured, elderly, or younger people struggle to perform independently. The design of our inflatable outfit could be a solution to assist them in these tasks, while the purpose of the hand pump was to boost their morale by allowing them to activate the robot manually by themselves. The experiments showed the performance of each type of flat tube actuator depending on its shape and curvature before attaching them to various garment parts according to their functionality. Finally, we test the outfit’s ability to change the shape of the armhole. The insight provided by this research might help improve the quality of life and independence in activities of daily living.
